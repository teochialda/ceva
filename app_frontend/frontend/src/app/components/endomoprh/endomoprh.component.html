Endomorph

  • Endomorphs are individuals with a naturally stocky and round body shape, characterized by higher levels of body fat and a slower metabolism.
  • People with an endomorph body type may find it easier to gain weight, particularly in the form of body fat, and may have a harder time losing weight.
  • Endomorphs often have a wider bone structure, shorter limbs, and a tendency to store excess weight in the midsection.
