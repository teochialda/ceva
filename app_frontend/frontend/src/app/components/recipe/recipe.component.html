Grilled Chicken Salad

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breast
  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Lemon vinaigrette dressing

Preparation: Grill the chicken breast until cooked thoroughly. Slice it into thin strips. Combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, and sliced cucumber in a bowl. Add the grilled chicken strips. Drizzle with lemon vinaigrette dressing and toss gently.

Steamed Fish with Vegetables

Ingredients:

  • White fish fillet
  • Broccoli florets
  • Carrots
  • Zucchini
  • Lemon juice

Preparation: Place the fish fillet in a steamer and steam until cooked. In a separate steamer, steam the broccoli florets, carrots, and zucchini until tender. Serve the steamed fish on a plate with the steamed vegetables. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the dish for added flavor.

Quinoa Stuffed Bell Peppers

Ingredients:

  • Bell peppers
  • Quinoa
  • Spinach
  • Feta cheese
  • Italian seasoning

Preparation: Cook quinoa according to package instructions. In a pan, sauté spinach until wilted. Mix cooked quinoa, sautéed spinach, crumbled feta cheese, and Italian seasoning in a bowl. Cut the tops off bell peppers, remove seeds, and stuff them with the quinoa mixture. Bake in the oven until the peppers are tender.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Mixed berries (e.g., strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • Almonds
  • Honey

Preparation: In a glass or bowl, layer Greek yogurt, mixed berries, and almonds. Drizzle with honey. Repeat the layers until desired amount is achieved. Serve chilled.

Ingredients:

  • Rolled oats
  • Almond milk
  • Ripe banana
  • Cinnamon powder

Grilled Vegetable Skewers

Ingredients:

  • Bell peppers
  • Zucchini
  • Red onion
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Balsamic glaze

Preparation: Cut bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion into chunks. Thread the vegetables onto skewers, alternating with cherry tomatoes. Grill the skewers until the vegetables are tender and slightly charred. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving.

Spinach and Mushroom Omelette

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Spinach
  • Mushrooms
  • Onion
  • Salt and pepper

Preparation: Beat eggs in a bowl and season with salt and pepper. Sauté spinach, mushrooms, and onion in a pan until cooked. Pour the beaten eggs over the vegetables and cook until the omelette is set. Fold it in half and serve.

Lentil Soup

Ingredients:

  • Lentils
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Vegetable broth

Preparation: Rinse lentils and cook them in vegetable broth according to package instructions. Chop carrots, celery, and onion and add them to the lentils. Simmer until the vegetables are tender and the flavors are well combined. Season with salt and pepper to taste.

Grilled Shrimp with Lemon and Garlic

Ingredients:

  • Shrimp
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic cloves
  • Olive oil
  • Fresh parsley

Preparation: In a bowl, combine shrimp, lemon juice, minced garlic cloves, and a drizzle of olive oil. Let the shrimp marinate for a few minutes. Grill the shrimp until cooked through. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
