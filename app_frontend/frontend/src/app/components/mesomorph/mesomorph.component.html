Mesomorph

  • Mesomorphs are individuals with a naturally athletic and muscular body shape, characterized by a well-defined physique and an ability to easily build and maintain muscle mass.
  • People with a mesomorph body type tend to have a moderate metabolism and may find it easier to both gain and lose weight compared to other body types.
  • Mesomorphs often have a well-proportioned body with broad shoulders, a narrow waist, and a muscular frame.
