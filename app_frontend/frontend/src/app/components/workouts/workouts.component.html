Push-ups

Push-ups are a classic bodyweight exercise that strengthen the chest, shoulders, and triceps muscles. They can be modified to suit different fitness levels and are an effective way to build upper body strength. Push-ups also engage the core muscles, promoting overall stability and balance. Incorporating push-ups into your workout routine can help improve muscular endurance and contribute to a well-rounded fitness program.

Pull-Ups

Pull-ups are a challenging upper body exercise that target the muscles of the back, shoulders, and arms. By gripping a horizontal bar and pulling your body weight up, pull-ups build strength and improve posture. They are an excellent compound exercise that helps develop a strong and defined upper body.

Squats

Squats are a fundamental lower body exercise that engage multiple muscle groups, including the quadriceps, hamstrings, glutes, and core. By squatting down and standing back up, squats help improve leg strength, stability, and overall functional movement. They are a versatile exercise that can be performed with or without weights and are beneficial for both athletes and fitness enthusiasts.
